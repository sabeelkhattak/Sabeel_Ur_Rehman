Nutrition

Proper nutrition is essential to support your fitness journey and maintain overall health. Here’s a balanced approach:

Nutrient Sources Benefits
Proteins Lean meats, eggs, beans, nuts Supports muscle repair and growth
Carbohydrates Whole grains, vegetables, fruits Provides energy for workouts
Fats Avocado, olive oil, nuts Essential for cell function and hormone production
Hydration Water Keeps you hydrated and supports bodily functions
Vitamins & Minerals Fruits, vegetables, nuts Supports immune health and overall well-being
